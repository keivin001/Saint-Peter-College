New Academic Year 2025/2026 Opens

May 15, 2025 St Peter College Administration 4 min read
  1. Home
  2. News & Events
  3. Academic Year 2025/2026

St Peter College of Shyogwe is thrilled to officially welcome all new and returning students to the 2025/2026 Academic Year — a year filled with promise, growth, and exciting opportunities.

The new academic year marks a significant milestone for our institution as we welcome over 650 students across all programs. With improved infrastructure, upgraded laboratories, and enhanced digital learning resources, this year promises to be one of our most transformative yet.

What's New This Academic Year

This year, St Peter College introduces several major upgrades to enhance the student experience and academic quality:

  • Renovated Computer Lab: Our ICT laboratory has been fully equipped with 60 new computers and high-speed internet connectivity, benefiting Software Development students in particular.
  • New Library Wing: An expanded library wing with over 3,000 new titles — including digital e-books and academic journals — is now accessible to all students.
  • Upgraded Science Facilities: Chemistry and Biology labs have received new equipment, improving practical learning for Education program students.
  • Student Welfare Center: A dedicated counselling and student welfare office has been opened to support student mental health and academic guidance.

Message from the Principal

"This academic year, we are more committed than ever to delivering excellence. Our investment in facilities and faculty development is a testament to our dedication to every student's success. Welcome back — let us make this year remarkable together."

— Dr. Jean Bosco, Principal, St Peter College of Shyogwe
