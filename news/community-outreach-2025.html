Community Outreach Program

  1. Home
  2. News & Events
  3. Community Outreach
Achievement August 20, 2025 Student Affairs Office 4 min read

More than 120 students and 15 staff members from St Peter College dedicated three days to community service across five villages in Muhanga District, reinforcing the college's commitment to social responsibility.

Giving Back to the Community

The Community Outreach Program, held from August 18–20, 2025, saw students from all four diploma programs working side by side with local community members. Activities ranged from free literacy classes and environmental clean-up campaigns to basic health awareness sessions and infrastructure repair work at a nearby primary school.

The program was organised by the St Peter College Student Association in partnership with the Muhanga District Social Affairs Office. It is part of the college's annual calendar and aligns with Rwanda's Umuganda tradition of community work and social cohesion.

Activities Undertaken

  • Free adult literacy classes in 3 villages (over 80 adults attended)
  • Repainted classrooms at Kamonyi Primary School
  • Planted 200 trees along the Muhanga–Kigali road
  • Distributed school supplies to 60 underprivileged children
  • Organised a health talk on hygiene and disease prevention
  • Basic computer literacy demonstrations for secondary school students

"Going into those villages reminded me why I chose the Education program. When you see children excited about learning, it changes everything. This is the impact we want to have."

— Nshimiyimana Patrick, 2nd Year Education Student

Impact and Recognition

The Muhanga District Mayor's office officially acknowledged the college's contribution through a letter of appreciation, commending the students for their exemplary service. The district also pledged to partner with St Peter College on future community development initiatives.

The outreach program reflects the college's core value of community — producing not just skilled graduates, but responsible citizens who actively contribute to the development of Rwanda.
