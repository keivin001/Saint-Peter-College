Graduation Ceremony 2025

  1. Home
  2. News & Events
  3. Graduation 2025
Events June 10, 2025 Admin, St Peter College 5 min read

St Peter College of Shyogwe celebrated the graduation of its Class of 2025 in a colourful and emotional ceremony held on June 10, 2025, at the college main campus in Muhanga District.

A Day of Celebration and Pride

Over 180 students donned their caps and gowns to receive their diplomas in Software Development, Accounting, Building Construction, and Education & Teaching. Family members, college staff, community leaders, and representatives from the Rwanda Education Board were in attendance to witness this momentous occasion.

The ceremony opened with a prayer and national anthem, followed by a procession of graduates into the main hall. The college choir performed two special pieces, setting a joyful tone for the entire event.

Best Student Awards

The ceremony included the presentation of Best Student Awards to outstanding graduates in each program. The Overall Best Graduate Award went to Ms. Uwase Clarisse of the Software Development program, who maintained a distinction throughout her two-year diploma.

"This diploma is not the end of learning — it is the beginning of your contribution to Rwanda. Go forth with integrity, with skill, and with the spirit of service."

— Dr. Jean Bosco, Principal, during his graduation address

Message to Graduates

The guest of honour, a representative from the Rwanda Education Board, encouraged graduates to embrace lifelong learning and contribute meaningfully to Rwanda's Vision 2050 development agenda. He praised St Peter College for its consistent output of skilled, work-ready graduates.

The ceremony concluded with a reception, photo sessions, and a cultural performance by the college dance troupe. It was a day full of joy, gratitude, and inspiration for students, families, and staff alike.
