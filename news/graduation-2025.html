Graduation Ceremony 2025

June 10, 2025 Events Office 5 min read
  1. Home
  2. News & Events
  3. Graduation 2025

On June 10, 2025, St Peter College of Shyogwe held its most memorable Graduation Ceremony to date, celebrating the achievements of 187 graduating students from the Class of 2025.

The ceremony took place at the St Peter College main campus grounds in Muhanga District, attended by graduates, their families, college faculty, government representatives, and community leaders. The atmosphere was filled with joy, pride, and a deep sense of accomplishment.

A Day of Celebration

The event began at 9:00 AM with a procession of graduates dressed in gowns and caps, accompanied by traditional Rwandan music performances from the college's cultural club. Distinguished guests included representatives from the Rwanda Education Board and the local government of Muhanga District.

Valedictorian Address

"We came here as students seeking knowledge. We leave as professionals ready to transform our communities. St Peter College gave us not just a diploma, but a foundation for life. We are grateful."

— Class of 2025 Valedictorian, Software Development

Principal's Remarks

Principal Dr. Jean Bosco congratulated the graduates and encouraged them to carry the values of integrity, excellence, and service they had learned at St Peter College into their professional careers. He reminded them that they represent the college wherever they go.

What Comes Next

Many graduates have already secured employment in their respective fields. The Accounting graduates have found placements in local businesses and NGOs, while Software Development graduates have been recruited by tech firms in Kigali. Building Construction graduates are contributing to Rwanda's infrastructure development, and Education graduates are joining schools across the country.

We are immensely proud of every member of the Class of 2025. Congratulations to all our graduates — the future is yours!
