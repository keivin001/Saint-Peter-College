New Academic Year 2025/2026 Opens

  1. Home
  2. News & Events
  3. Academic Year 2025/2026
Announcement May 15, 2025 Admin, St Peter College 4 min read

St Peter College of Education is proud to announce the official opening of the 2025/2026 Academic Year — a milestone that welcomes a new generation of students into our community of learning, growth, and excellence.

A New Chapter Begins

The 2025/2026 academic year marks a significant step forward for St Peter College. After months of preparation, renovation, and curriculum review, our institution opens its doors with enhanced facilities, expanded programs, and a renewed commitment to student success.

Over the past year, the college has invested significantly in upgrading its digital classrooms, expanding the computer laboratory, and renovating student accommodation blocks to provide a more comfortable and productive learning environment for all enrolled students.

What's New This Year

  • Fully upgraded ICT laboratory with 60 new workstations
  • New digital projectors and smart boards in all classrooms
  • Expanded library collection with over 500 new titles
  • Improved Wi-Fi coverage across the entire campus
  • Introduction of three new short-course programs
  • New student counselling and career guidance centre

Message from the Principal

"This academic year represents our strongest commitment yet to the students of Rwanda. We have listened to feedback, invested in our infrastructure, and assembled an exceptional team of educators. We welcome every student to a year of discovery, hard work, and achievement."

— Dr. Jean Bosco, Principal, St Peter College of Education

Orientation Programme

New students joined us for a week-long orientation that covered academic expectations, campus tour, library induction, student welfare services, and an introduction to all four diploma programs. The programme also included team-building activities and a welcome dinner hosted by the student association.

Returning students were welcomed back with a refresher session on updated academic policies, new grading criteria, and information on scholarship opportunities available for the new year.
