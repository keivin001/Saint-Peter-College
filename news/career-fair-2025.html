Career Fair & Employer Exhibition 2025

  1. Home
  2. News & Events
  3. Career Fair 2025
Events October 5, 2025 Career Services Office 4 min read

St Peter College hosted its annual Career Fair and Employer Exhibition on October 5, 2025, connecting over 200 students with 18 top employers from across Rwanda's private and public sectors.

Bridging Students and Industry

The Career Fair, held in the college's main multipurpose hall, provided final-year diploma students with a unique opportunity to meet face-to-face with potential employers, explore job openings, discuss internship opportunities, and gain insight into what industry professionals look for in new graduates.

Employers represented sectors including banking and finance, construction and engineering, technology companies, education institutions, NGOs, and government agencies. Many attendees came specifically to recruit from St Peter College's graduating class.

Participating Employers

  • Bank of Kigali – Graduate Trainee Programme
  • Rwanda Revenue Authority – ICT Department
  • Inyenyeri Technologies – Junior Developer Roles
  • RSSB – Finance and Accounting Graduates
  • Muhanga District – Education Officers
  • World Vision Rwanda – Community Development Officers
  • Various construction firms – Site Supervisors

Career Guidance Sessions

Alongside the exhibition, the college ran three parallel career guidance sessions covering CV writing, interview preparation, and professional networking. These sessions were facilitated by HR professionals from participating companies and were attended by over 150 students.

"I walked in nervous and walked out with two interview invitations. The employers here are genuinely looking for talent from colleges like St Peter. It was a real confidence booster."

— Muhimpundu Aline, Final Year Accounting Student

Outcomes

By the end of the day, 23 students had received on-the-spot interview offers and 47 had successfully submitted their CVs for consideration. The college's Career Services Office will continue to follow up with both students and employers throughout October and November to facilitate successful placements.
