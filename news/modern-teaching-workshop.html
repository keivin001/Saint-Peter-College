Workshop on Modern Teaching Methods

  1. Home
  2. News & Events
  3. Teaching Workshop
Workshop July 5, 2025 Academic Department 4 min read

St Peter College faculty members participated in a transformative professional development workshop focused on modern, student-centred teaching methods and technology-integrated pedagogy.

About the Workshop

Held over three days from July 3–5, 2025, the workshop brought together all 45 teaching staff members from across all four academic departments. The sessions were facilitated by expert trainers from the Rwanda Education Board's Teacher Training Department and a visiting lecturer from the University of Rwanda.

The workshop was structured around four core themes: active learning strategies, technology integration in classrooms, inclusive education, and assessment for learning.

Key Topics Covered

  • Flipped classroom and blended learning models
  • Using digital tools: Google Classroom, Kahoot, Padlet
  • Project-based learning and collaborative group work
  • Differentiated instruction for diverse learners
  • Formative vs. summative assessment best practices
  • Supporting students with learning challenges

Staff Reflections

"The workshop completely changed how I think about lesson planning. I used to focus on covering content — now I focus on what students are actually doing with the content. The practical sessions were especially eye-opening."

— Mr. Emmanuel Habimana, Software Development Lecturer

Many staff members highlighted the hands-on nature of the sessions as the most valuable aspect. Rather than sitting through lectures, they practised the very techniques they were learning — experiencing first-hand what engaged, interactive learning feels like from a student's perspective.

Implementation Plan

Following the workshop, each department committed to implementing at least two new teaching strategies per semester. The Director of Studies will conduct classroom observation visits in the first month to support staff in applying their new skills, and a follow-up session is scheduled for October 2025 to share experiences and lessons learned.

St Peter College remains committed to continuous professional development for all teaching staff as part of its strategy to deliver the highest quality education to its students.
