New Short Courses Launched

  1. Home
  2. News & Events
  3. New Short Courses
Announcement September 1, 2025 Academic Department 3 min read

St Peter College of Shyogwe officially launches three new short-course programs designed to upskill working professionals, school leavers, and community members in high-demand fields.

Responding to Market Demand

Following extensive market research and consultation with industry partners, the college has developed three new short courses that directly address skills gaps in Rwanda's growing economy. These courses are designed to be accessible, practical, and immediately applicable in the workplace.

The Three New Courses

Digital Marketing

Learn social media marketing, SEO, content creation, and online advertising strategies.

3 Months
Cybersecurity Basics

Understand threats, data protection, safe browsing, and basic network security principles.

4 Months
English for Work

Build professional English communication skills for interviews, reports, and workplace interaction.

3 Months

Who Can Enrol?

  • Secondary school leavers (S3 minimum)
  • Working professionals seeking to upskill
  • Entrepreneurs and small business owners
  • Government and NGO employees

Classes are offered in both weekday and weekend formats to accommodate learners with different schedules. Course fees are kept affordable, and scholarship support is available for qualifying students.

To register or get more information, contact the admissions office or visit our Programs page.
