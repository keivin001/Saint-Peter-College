ABOUT US

Saint Peter College of Shyogwe

St Peter College of Education (Saint Peter College of Shyogwe) is a premier institution located in the heart of Muhanga District, Rwanda. We are committed to fostering academic excellence, vocational skills, and strong moral values among our students.

Our history is rooted in a deep commitment to community development and providing accessible, high-quality education to the youth of Rwanda.

Mission

To provide quality education and skills training that empowers students to become competent, responsible, and innovative leaders in their communities.

Vision

To be a center of excellence in education and vocational training, recognized for producing world-class graduates who drive socio-economic development in Rwanda and beyond.
