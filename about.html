Our Story

Saint Peter College of Shyogwe

St Peter College of Education was established with a steadfast mission to provide quality education, teacher training and professional development programs that empower individuals to make a positive impact in their communities.

Over the years, we have built a rich reputation for academic excellence, discipline, and innovation. Our graduates are found across Rwanda and beyond, contributing meaningfully to social and economic development.

Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To provide quality education and professional training that empowers students with competence, ethical values, and innovative thinking — creating professionals who address social and economic challenges with excellence.

Our Vision

To be a leading institution in education and professional development, producing graduates who are excellence-driven, innovative, and technically capable — contributing to Rwanda's transformation and development.
