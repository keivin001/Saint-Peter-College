What Our Alumni Say

Real stories from graduates who studied at St Peter College and went on to build careers that transform their communities.

"St Peter College gave me far more than a diploma. The Software Development program taught me real coding skills, and within three months of graduating I secured a job at a tech company in Kigali. I owe my career to this institution."

Mugisha Eric
Software Development, Class of 2023

"The teachers here genuinely care about your success. I came from a rural background with no connections, and the college opened every door for me. I now work as an accountant at a government ministry and I'm proud to say St Peter College is where it all started."

Uwimana Clarisse
Accounting, Class of 2022 · Best Graduate

"The Building Construction program was hands-on from day one. We didn't just study theory — we built real things. I graduated and immediately started my own small construction company. St Peter College gave me the confidence and the skills to be my own boss."

Habimana Jean
Building Construction, Class of 2023

"I chose the Education program because I wanted to make a difference in young children's lives. St Peter College trained me not just to teach, but to inspire. I now teach at a primary school in Huye and my students are my greatest achievement."

Murebwayire Aline
Education & Teaching, Class of 2024

"The facilities are excellent and the lecturers are highly qualified. What surprised me most was how much practical experience we got. By the time I graduated, I already had a portfolio of web projects that impressed every employer I interviewed with."

Software Development, Class of 2024

"As a parent, choosing the right college for my son was the most important decision I made. St Peter College exceeded all our expectations — the discipline, the values, the teaching quality. My son graduated and now works at a bank. We are forever grateful."
